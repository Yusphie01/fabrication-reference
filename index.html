Heat Input (kJ/mm) = (Voltage √ó Amperage √ó 60) / (1000 √ó Travel Speed)

Welding Defects & Quality Control

Common Welding Defects - ISO 6520

1. Cracks (ISO 6520 Group 1)

Types:

  • Hot Crack: Solidification crack, centerline crack
  • Cold Crack: Hydrogen-induced crack, toe crack
  • Lamellar Tearing: Through-thickness crack

Prevention: Preheat, low hydrogen electrode, proper WPS

CriticalNot Acceptable

2. Porosity (ISO 6520 Group 2)

Types:

  • Uniform Porosity: Gas bubbles distributed
  • Cluster Porosity: Localized group
  • Wormhole: Elongated gas cavity

Prevention: Dry electrode, clean surface, proper gas flow
